Materials & Craftsmanship

Materials Used

At Prism Frames, every artwork is created with carefully selected materials to ensure premium finish, lasting beauty, and dimensional depth.

Core Materials

Premium Glass

Clear, high-quality glass is used as the main surface to provide a clean, refined look and enhance the layered visual effect.

Acrylic Colors

Professional acrylic paints are applied for rich pigmentation, sharp detail, and long-lasting color performance.

Metallic Sheets

Stainless steel and decorative metallic sheets are used to add shine, texture, and a luxury finish to selected artworks.

Adhesive Layers

Strong bonding materials help secure each layer properly, making the artwork durable and neatly assembled.

Protective Backing

A solid backing layer supports the artwork structure and improves the stability of the final frame.

Why These Materials Matter

  • They improve depth and layered presentation.
  • They help maintain color and finish over time.
  • They create a premium and luxurious appearance.
  • They support durability for long-term display.

Craftsmanship Promise

Each piece is assembled by hand with attention to detail, balance, and finish so that the final work feels both artistic and refined.
