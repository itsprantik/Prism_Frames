Who We Are

Art for collectors who want more than a flat print.

We started Prism Frames with a simple idea: wall art should feel immersive. Every piece is designed to shift with light, angle, and distance so it keeps revealing something new inside the room.

Our process is collaborative from the first message to the final piece. Whether the subject is automotive, anime, landscape, or a personal concept, we translate it into a custom layered composition that feels premium and precise.

Studio Principle
