Our Process

How we turn a chosen image into a finished Prism Frames piece.

Our workflow is built around print precision, material compatibility, and clean hand-finishing so every commission feels custom, durable, and display-ready.

Workflow Overview

Printed on transfer sheet, applied to the requested surface, and finished by hand.

Depending on the design and size, we print the artwork on transfer sheets in A5, A4, or larger custom layouts. For bigger pieces, we can use three or more A4 transfer parts before applying the work onto glass or metal as requested by the customer.

01

Choose The Design

Artwork, reference, and output size are finalized.

We first confirm the selected image or artwork reference, then decide the required print format in A5, A4, or a custom size based on the final piece.

02

Print Preparation

The image is printed onto transfer sheet.

The design is prepared carefully for transfer printing. For larger custom work, the image can be split across three or more A4 transfer sheets for accurate coverage.

03

Material Selection

The print is matched to glass or metal.

Once printed, the transfer is applied to the material requested by the customer, usually glass or metal, depending on the desired final look and finish.

04

Assembly & Finish

The full piece is aligned, layered, and refined.

We complete the work by aligning each part properly, finishing the surface, and checking the overall presentation so the final artwork looks clean and premium.

Typical Formats

Flexible sizing for different commissions

A5

Ideal for compact pieces, gifting, or smaller accent displays.

A4

A strong standard size for detailed artwork and balanced wall placement.

Custom Multi-Part

For larger works, we combine three or more A4 transfer sections into one custom final composition.
